Camera Properties

The Camera type exposes the following members.

Properties
  NameDescription
FarClipPlane
The far clip plane of the perspective matrix. Updates all matrices when set.
FovDegrees
The vertical field of view in degrees. Updates FovRadians and all matrices when set.

Values less than or equal to 0 or greater than or equal to 180 are ignored.

FovRadians
The vertical field of view in radians. Updates FovDegrees and all matrices when set.

Values less than or equal to 0 or greater than or equal to PI are ignored.

ModelViewMatrix
The result of RotationMatrix * TranslationMatrix
MvpMatrix
The result of ModelViewMatrix * PerspectiveMatrix
NearClipPlane
The near clip plane of the perspective matrix. Updates all matrices when set.
PerspectiveMatrix
The result of CreatePerspectiveFieldOfView(Single, Single, Single, Single) for FovRadians, renderWidth / renderHeight, NearClipPlane, FarClipPlane
Position
The position of the camera in scene units. Updates all matrices when set.
RotationMatrix
The result of CreateRotationY(Single) * CreateRotationX(Single)
RotationXDegrees
The rotation around the x-axis in degrees. Updates rotationXRadians and all matrices when set.
RotationXRadians
The rotation around the x-axis in radians. Updates RotationXDegrees and all matrices when set.
RotationYDegrees
The rotation around the y-axis in degrees. Updates all matrices when set.
RotationYRadians
The rotation around the y-axis in radians. Updates all matrices when set.
TranslationMatrix
The result of CreateTranslation(Single, Single, Single) for X, -Y, Z of Position
